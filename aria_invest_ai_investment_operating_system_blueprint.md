# 1. Vision

ARIA INVEST is not just a stock tracker.

It is an:
- AI-powered investment intelligence system
- explainable recommendation engine
- portfolio risk management platform
- sector rotation analyzer
- monthly wealth operating system
- disciplined investing framework

The goal is NOT:
- gambling
- hyperactive intraday trading
- unrealistic return chasing
- predicting every market movement

The goal IS:
- disciplined investing
- systematic decision-making
- controlled risk
- explainable investment recommendations
- monthly compounding
- passive wealth generation
- capital protection

# 2. Core Investment Philosophy

The platform must follow these principles:

## 2.1 Capital Protection First

Protecting capital is more important than maximizing profit.

## 2.2 Probability Over Prediction

The system should improve probability.
Not attempt perfect prediction.

## 2.3 Monthly Compounding Approach

Target realistic and sustainable monthly growth.

Recommended ranges:

| Style | Expected Monthly Range |
|---|---|
| Conservative | 2-4% |
| Balanced | 4-6% |
| Aggressive | 6-8% |

## 2.4 Controlled Greed

System should enforce:
- profit booking
- stop losses
- allocation discipline
- diversification
- risk reduction

## 2.5 Explainable AI

Every recommendation MUST explain:
- why this stock
- why now
- risk factors
- sector reasoning
- institutional activity
- strategy
