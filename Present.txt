### 🏆 Pro-Tips for Delivering This Script

1. **The "Pause" is Your Best Friend:** After you say *"look at this number: $12,300 in automated savings,"* **stop talking for 2 full seconds**. Let the judges' eyes find the number on the screen. Silence builds anticipation and makes the number feel heavier.
 in the "Wow" Moment:** When describing the Optimization Engine, use your hands. *"ARIA intercepts... evaluates... and dynamically routes."* Physical gestures make the invisible software process feel tangible and powerful.
3. **The Backup Plan:** If the live optimization demo fails or loads slowly, do not panic and do not apologize profusely. Immediately say: *"To ensure we respect the judges' time and show the exact flow, I’m going to switch to a 30-second pre-recorded clip of this optimization happening."* Then play your backup video. Judges will respect your preparedness more than they will penalize a minor tech glitch.
4. **Rehearse with a Timer:** Read this script out loud *while clicking through your actual slides*. If it takes longer than 5 minutes and 30 seconds, you are speaking too fast or lingering too long on clicks. Trim the fat, not the impact.

You have a incredibly strong narrative here. Deliver this with confidence, and the FinOps value proposition will be impossible for the judges to ignore. Good luck!
